# {title}
This is my personal website built with SvelteKit. It serves as a portfolio to showcase my projects and provide contact information. Building this site was also an opportunity for me to learn a new frontend framework.

## Why SvelteKit?
I chose SvelteKit for its simplicity and performance. This website is hosted on a VPS (Virtual Private Server) with limited resources, and SvelteKit's efficient rendering and minimal overhead make it ideal for such environments.

I've built projects with Next.js before, but found it too heavy for my VPS and the overall experience felt sluggish. SvelteKit, in contrast, feels snappy and lightweight.

## Inspiration
This website is heavily inspired by [rootkid's website](https://rootkid.me/). I'm not particularly skilled at design, so I drew inspiration from his clean, minimalistic approach.

The color scheme I used is [Catppuccin](https://catppuccin.com/), which I'm a big fan of.
